Privacy Policy - Calculadora Fácil

Last updated: September 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of the application Calculadora Fácil, developed by TriVisionTech, based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect user information when using the application.

2. Data Collection

The application Calculadora Fácil does not collect personally identifiable information. All operations and calculations are performed locally on the user's device.

3. Permissions

The app may request basic device permissions, such as storage access, only to allow local saving of calculations. No information is sent to external servers.

4. Advertising

The application Calculadora Fácil may display ads provided by advertising partners. These ads may collect anonymous data, such as device identifiers or usage behavior, solely for personalization and performance analysis. For more details, please refer to the privacy policy of the advertising providers.

5. Data Usage

The data collected by advertising partners is anonymous and does not personally identify the user. TriVisionTech does not sell, share, or distribute any personal information to third parties.

6. Changes to this Policy

TriVisionTech may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend that users review this page regularly to stay informed about any changes.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or about the Calculadora Fácil application, please contact us by email: faturamaenterprise@gmail.com.
